Software Recommendations

I don't know many unique programs that other people don't recommend, but seeing as I use w*ndows 10 for the majority of my time on a computer, I may know a few offhand that you'd otherwise have to search for: [not all 100% libre, but neither is w10]

  • nvim - the best text editor
  • 7-Zip - a very good zipping multitool (has gui too (thats bad))
  • JPEGView - a very minimalist image viewer
  • MPC-HC(homepage) - a quite minimalist music player (uses windows-style command line flags lol)
  • mpv - probably the best video viewer
  • NirCMD - multitool but VERY indian, be warned
  • git - read the site
  • github-cli aka gh - self-explanatory
  • sumatra pdf - best w*ndows pdf viewer
  • github-cli aka gh - best lignux pdf viewer
  • youtube-dl - video downloader from basically every site
